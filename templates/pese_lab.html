Lab Summaries

Lab 1

Introduction & Communication Skills

In today's lab, ma'am observed our gestures, postures, and personalities. At first, she seemed strict, but once she started speaking, I was impressed—especially by her diverse academic background. She also shared some stories, which made the session more engaging. Then she shared some incidents of her with us and also narrated some tales. She changed my perspective of never have a single opinion of anyone without talking or knowing them. She has kind of personality that fills everyone with enthusiasm, & have a supportive nature and creates a motivated and learning environment — a personality that every career skills teacher must possess. At last we played a really interesting game named Chinese Whispers (also known as Telephone) in which she made us stand in a queue with semi circle and then whispered a quote/phrase, and we suppose to pass the exact words to the next person, the last person listened a completely new phrase far from the word, which teaches us the following:

Activity: Chinese Whisper

We played an interesting game called Chinese Whisper where we had to pass a phrase along a queue, demonstrating how messages can change through miscommunication.

Key Learning:

  • Effective listening skills
  • Miscommunication can lead to big blunders
Lab 2

Personal Appearance & CV Making

In today's session ma'am taught us about the outer appearance of a person which is the first impression of a person. She gave some examples where a person's appearance can make or break their chances of success.She also given some examples of students and their differently wore attire i.e. uniform — some had not tuck their shirt in, some with open fizzy hairs, some having sleeves rolled up and other faults. Then she asked who think himself/herself to be properly dressed and correctly. I stood only — stood up in class of 80 students with full confidence because I always knew the importance of wear carrying formal dress, it directly tells our personality. After that ma'am shifted to new topic i.e. how the design a CV with all necessary detail. Then ma'am conducted an activity in which she gave the instruction to tear page edges accordingly i.e. fold, tear right edge, left edge etc. At last ma'am told us, asked everyone to show their pages & all the pages had different crafty designs.

Activity: Paper Folding Exercise

An activity where we had to tear page edges according to instructions, demonstrating how the same instructions can lead to different results.

Key Learning:

  • Our appearance makes a strong impression
  • Active listening is important
Lab 3

Soft Skills & Attitude

In today's session mam told about the importance of soft skills, how soft skills help us to get ahead from others and how it helps in creating positive impact on others.First thing was about soft skills — that how our soft skills keep us ahead from others, how it helps in creating positive impact to others. She shared a story that how the father to boys whose they were talking about cars — then how boy negotiated for car but told her a day before that she can afford it for free. But during conversation due to lack of soft skills, how boy ruined the conversation! After that we did an activity, where ma'am wants to show us that how luck, skills, hardwork and right attitude plays role in our life — where even if you are hardworking, you have a best luck and even money but with right attitude there is no use of them, you can't achieve everything in life without right attitude.

Activity: Success Factors

An activity showing how luck, skill, hardwork and right attitude play important roles in our life.

Key Learning:

  • Right attitude is very important in life
  • Soft skills are crucial for success
Lab 4

Integrity & Facing Challenges

Today mam was really upset as some students cheated in the mid term practical. She emphasized that cheating in life would never help us as it would only make us coward not stronger.Then ma'am told us a story of her husband when she motivated him for a daring task – this doesn't mean she don't care about the task she want why die being coward – die being courageous & fearless. After that mam divided us in groups & given us a situation "You work in a company and attending a meeting you encountered one of your colleagues stinking badly. How would you handle this situation, without making awkward to the person."

Activity: Role Play

Group activity on handling sensitive situations in a professional environment.

Key Learning:

  • Always face the challenges never run away from them
  • Integrity is crucial in professional life
Lab 5

Group Discussion Skills

Today's session was all about Group Discussion. At first ma'am taught us the difference between group discussion, debate, relay communication, presentation & storytelling. She taught us how to do any group discussion. As I really like taking part in GDs and debates so it really helped me. And I noted some key points like – if you want to be the leader of your group in GD, start with impactful, powerful starting of the topic given. You have to be exceptional & your voice should be phenomenal so that without disrespecting your fellow colleagues you deliver your thoughts & your conclusion shouldn't be according to your personal opinion – it should be crisp, to the point & according to majority approval. Then ma'am taught us about relay communication, and divided the class into 2 groups. One was given the topic "Is US still a superpower?" and other groups were given the topic "Civic sense is missing in Indian people". Everyone spoke really well. I get to know everyone's perspective, their opinion, it eventually gained my knowledge in these topics.

Activity: Group Discussions

Practical GD sessions on various topics to practice communication and leadership skills.

Key Learning:

  • Team work and collaboration
  • Critical thinking
  • Active listening
